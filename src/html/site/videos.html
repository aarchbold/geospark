What is Hyperion?

Hyperion’s trained machine learning algorithms analyze information from breaking news, social media, space and IoT channels and continually updates global stability and activity levels at country, region and city levels. Hyperion’s artificial intelligence defines patterns of normal human activity across the globe and alerts users to abnormal patterns informing organizations what is happening and why.
